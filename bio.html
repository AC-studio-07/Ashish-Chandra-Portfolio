About

Ashish Chandra is a photographer based in Dehradun, India. Working across documentary, portraiture, and personal narrative, his practice is rooted in observation — quiet, slow, and deeply felt. His images often explore the subtle tension between presence and absence, with a strong emphasis on emotional truth.
